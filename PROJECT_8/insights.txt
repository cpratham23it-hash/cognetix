Identified Customer Segments

a) High-Income, High-Spending Customers (VIP Segment)
1. Most valuable customers
2. High purchasing power
3. Ideal for premium offers and loyalty programs

b) High-Income, Low-Spending Customers
1. Potential to upsell
2. Require personalized marketing strategies

c) Low-Income, High-Spending Customers
1. Price-sensitive but frequent buyers
2. Respond well to discounts and bundle offers

d) Low-Income, Low-Spending Customers (Budget Segment)
1. Least profitable
2. Suitable for basic or mass-market promotions
